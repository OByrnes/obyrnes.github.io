TailTracker

Dog Exercise Tracking App

Save, Review and organize your podcasts and discover new podcasts.

Add all your dogs

Add all your dogs. See recommendations based on their breed and age for Exercise and track their activities.

Add your Routes

Make a custom route to track your dog walks; Even if you go off the beaten-path.

Make a schedule for your walks according to the day of the week.

Adds activities according to your schedule to your dog's planner for the month.

Add Activity

Add an activity to your dog's schedule, add photos and comments to that activity and track if your dog's activities meet their daily exercise goal.

Breed information

Get information on dog breeds. See their breed-group, photo and temperament as well as how active they are.
